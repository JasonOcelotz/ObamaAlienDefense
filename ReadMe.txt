BOOT-UP: 



Double click aliendefense.exe and the
game should run.


------------------------------------------------------

WIDESCREEN + HD GRAPHICS: 



At the top of the screen you will see a
navigation bar, click on the "View" text. 
A drop-down menu should pop up. Click "Show All".

Then, click the "View" text at the top again, 
and click "Full Screen". 

Your game should now be 

NOTE: There will be a black 
bar on the right side of the screen, however 
this will ONLY appear in cut-scenes and in the menus.


------------------------------------------------------

CONTROLS: 


W = Jump

Up Arrow = Jump

S = Move Below

Down Arrow = Move Below

A = Move Left

Left Arrow = Move Left

D = Move Right

Right Arrow = Move Right

Z = Shoot

Left CTRL = Shoot
